About

Founded in 2008, the Ladysmith Camera Club serves as a dedicated hub for photography enthusiasts in the greater Ladysmith, British Columbia area. At its core, the club aims to kindle a deep appreciation for the photographic arts and create a space for the exchange of creative ideas within the community.

The Ladysmith Camera Club's mission revolves around cultivating a profound appreciation for photography and its artistic potential. Through exhibitions, workshops, and collaborative projects, the club actively encourages its members and the local community to explore the world through a photographic lens. Furthermore, the club is committed to sharing the photographic arts with the broader community, engaging with local schools, organizations, and events, and inspiring the next generation of photographers while showcasing the creative talents of its members. This approach reflects the club's commitment to uniting, inspiring, and capturing the beauty of Ladysmith and its people through the art of photography.

Who.

We are a passionate group of individuals dedicated to the art of photography. Our members come from diverse backgrounds and experiences, but we all share a common love for capturing moments through the lens. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, there's a place for you in our photography club. We believe in the power of collaboration and mutual support to help each other grow as artists.

When.

Our club holds regular meetings on the 4th Tuesday of every month from January through November. These gatherings take place at 7:00 pm and are open to anyone who has an interest in photography. Whether you're a club member or a first-time visitor, you're welcome to join us. Please note that there is a nominal $5 drop-in fee for non-members, which helps support our club's activities and resources.

What.

Our club's mission is to foster a nurturing environment for photographers to learn, explore, and enhance their craft. We organize a variety of activities and events throughout the year, including workshops, photo walks, and photo exhibitions. These opportunities allow us to delve into the art and science of photography, from composition and lighting to post-processing techniques. Our shared passion for photography binds us together, and we are committed to pushing our creative boundaries.

How.

Our approach to achieving our mission involves creating a space where photographers can learn, collaborate, and evolve. We encourage active participation and knowledge sharing among our members. We facilitate this through a combination of regular meetings, workshops, and online platforms for discussion and resource sharing. Our members can connect, exchange ideas, and support each other on their photographic journeys. We believe that by learning and growing together, we can elevate our skills and develop a deeper appreciation for the art of photography.
