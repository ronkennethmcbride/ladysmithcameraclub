Grizzlies are Saving Toba

Photoshoot - Linda McBride


The Toba Inlet Grizzly tour was amazing. Tourist dollars go to help the Klahoose save this area. Ice blue glacier waters meander through old growth forests with endless mountains views. Photographing a grizzly I switch from Aperature to Shutter and reset my exposure. I can hear them breathing as I watch them fish and lumber about from a safe shelter with two Klahoose guides.
